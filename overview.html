Characters

First of all when you start a game you must each pick a fighter, each of these fighters are unique in their play style and moveset. Most of these characters are from Nintendo owned games but recently more and more are from 3rd Parties. after the game starts you each battle to knock the other off the stage (basically a floating island). This island has 3 platfroms that you can land on but you can also fall through them with 2 downward inputs. You have moves to help you get back onstage but if you are knocked to far away (down,up, or right/left) you "die". These deathzones are just off-screen and are referred to as "blast-zones." Your goal is the launch the opponents into these blast-zones and to not get launched yourself. You only have a set amount of lives and whoever lasts the longest wins!
